Virtual Campus & Classroom

Let's talk about what our virtual campus has to offer. The virtual campus is where you will access your classes, financial aid documents, our learning center and student resources including the library, books, and tutoring. To get familiar with the virtual campus, we offer weekly live tours that you can join.

CTU's curriculum is designed to challenge you to organize, research and solve real-world problems. We refer to this as our Professional Learning Model. When classes begin, you'll have live or recorded live chats and assignments each week. Your assignments may be a discussion board, an individual project, a group project, or intellipath® (for COS: worksheets, presentation, simulations, tests). Intellipath® is our proprietary educational technology, which is an adaptive learning platform. Have you ever heard of that? Adaptive learning technology recognizes what you already know, anticipates what you're ready to learn, and personalizes your course materials accordingly.

ONL: Before classes start, you'll go through Orientation which gives you a chance to get familiar with the virtual classroom and the various types of assignments.

CTU Messenger & CTU Student Community

It's important for students to stay connected with CTU. One way to do that is with CTU Messenger. This is a two-way communication platform that allows you to easily chat with your advisors, instructors, and other departments. A lot of our students like the convenience of this tool because they may not be able to call us but can still get questions answered. It's easily accessible from the mobile app and a computer. Are you comfortable using instant messaging?

Another way for students to connect is with our CTU Student Community. This is a social media platform that connects you with other CTU students based on common interests.

Recognition & Graduation

I want to also share with you some of our recognition. CTU's online degree programs are ranked among the best in 2025 by U.S. News & World Report, which is a globally respected organization and authority in education rankings. 2025 was our eleventh consecutive year for this ranking.

Something else our students are often curious about is if we have a graduation ceremony. CTU has in-person and virtual graduation ceremonies.
